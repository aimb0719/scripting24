Anna Isabel Bolor is a first-year student in the Multimedia Production program focused on Graphic Design and Digital Video at Lethbridge Polytechnic.

Anna was born and raised in the Philippines. She earned her Bachelor's degree in Information Technology back in 2010. Before moving to Canada, Anna lived and worked in Dubai for 6 years for different multinational companies. The highlight of her career started when she was employed by one of the prestigious real estate developers in the UAE and received the Star Performer employee award in 2022.

Anna is the third and only girl among her siblings. Since she was young, Anna has always loved drawing, colouring, designing artworks, and taking photographs in her spare time. She made her first ever drawing in 2005, when she found some old W.I.T.C.H magazines at home. She started sketching them and was surprised that she got to draw the characters. Since then, she has always been interested in making something creative that inspired her to enroll in the program to redefine her skills.

When she is outside of school, Anna enjoys being a homebody. She loves watching vlogs and movies, especially horror films and documentaries, reading books on her kindle, and listening to music. She loves to sing her heart out in karaoke and listens to any kind of music depending on her mood. When she gets bored at home, she normally goes out for a walk and grabs a nice cup of coffee. Coffee is her blood type, and she thinks coffee can somehow solve her problems.
